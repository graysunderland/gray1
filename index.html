I work side by side with founders, product leads, and engineering teams to design interfaces that feel inevitable — calm under pressure, fast to use, and built to last beyond the next funding round.

The work spans early-stage 0→1 product design, design system architecture for scaling teams, and reshaping complex flows for teams that have outgrown their first interface. I care about the small mechanical decisions that compound into trust.
